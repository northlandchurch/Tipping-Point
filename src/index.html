Where Are You On The Journey?

Start Giving

A Step of Trust

This is a step of trust. We know that there are many people who love Northland and participate regularly in the life of the church, but do not yet give financially to the work of the church. If that’s you, we want you to know that we are glad that you are here and we want to invite you to begin to give back to God a portion of what he has given to you. The amount is not nearly as important as the spiritual step of trusting God with your finances.

Consistent Giving

A Step of Intentionality

This is a step of intentionality. When something has captivated our hearts - whether it be relationship, possession, experience, or cause - we naturally dedicate our resources toward it. If you give occasionally, we want to encourage you to give in a more intentional and consistent way. Ask the Lord, “What portion of what you have given to me do you want me to invest in the Kingdom in a consistent and faithful way?”

Tithing

A Step of Faithfulness

This is a step of faithfulness.  This is where we physically, practically demonstrate our belief that God will keep his word to provide for our needs.  In a culture that celebrates spending to 0, to set aside 10% of our income first requires us to go against the stream.  This will require that you reprioritize your spending and lifestyle choices to put God first before meeting personal needs. We do this not because God needs our money... rather he called his people to give to put Him first and experience joy, peace and blessing.

Extravagant Giving

A Step of Celebration

This is a step of celebration. This is a moment of recognizing that God allows us to be the conduit of blessing to the world. If you are tithing, remember that this principle of giving 10% of your income back to God was never intended to be a finish line. Perhaps the Lord wants you to excel still more in your giving. Prayerfully consider if God is asking you to increase your giving as a sacrifice for this vision.

For Everyone

Resource Giving

Regardless of which step God is calling you to, there may also be possessions that God has blessed you with so that you may be a blessing to others. Whether we feel that we are wealthy or not, we should all consider a special gift Prayerfully consider if there are some assets (savings, investments, property, boat,car...) God may be calling you to liquidate as a catalyst to launch this movement forward.
